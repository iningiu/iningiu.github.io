  1. at times: 有时

  2. in ages: for a long time
    eg. I haven’t seen you in ages. = I haven’t seen you in really long time.
    ​ I have been studying English for ages.

  3. run into: meeting someone accidentally
    eg. I ran into my friend in DHU the other day.

  4. lose it: lose control of your emotions; show strong,negative emotions
    eg. He completely lost it when I told him the terrible thing.

  5. freak out: show a lot of emotion;be not able to control emotions
    eg. She started freaking out when she saw a mouse run under her chair.

  6. 问候语：How is it going? = How are you?
    Things are going great. = I’m doing well. = Great. = It’s great.

  1. pad: a place where a person lives; apartment
    eg. Your pad is really far out.

  2. far out: the latest; cool

  3. crash: sleep
    eg. I lost my keys, so, I’m crashing at my girlfriend’s house.

  4. groovy: very cool

  5. You dig what I’m saying?
    dig: appreciate, like

  6. Last weekend, I throwed a birthday party at my home.
    Let throw a bash to celebrate your engagement.
    throw: host
    bash: a big party.

  7. I have to go to city hall today and take care of some business.
